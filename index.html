Information for navigating ALA Midwinter 2017 in Atlanta.

Below you will find a Google Map with loads of information - official conference locations and hotels, as well as local things to do, restaurants, and public transportation. On this site, you will also find social schedules and taxi info. If you have any suggestions for things to add either to the map/and or to this site, Tweet @kmapesy, and your suggestions will be included!
